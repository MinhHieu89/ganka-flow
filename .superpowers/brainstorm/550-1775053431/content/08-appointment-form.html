Appointment Form Behavior

  • Search first — type phone/name, autocomplete dropdown shows matches
  • Existing patient — green banner, read-only card, doctor defaults to last visit's doctor
  • New patient — yellow banner, editable fields (name, phone, reason required), creates temp patient record
  • Calendar — month view, Mondays struck through (clinic closed), past dates disabled
  • Time slots — morning/afternoon groups, 30-min intervals, "đầy" for full slots
  • Confirmation bar — summary for lễ tân to read to patient on phone before confirming
